Our Idea

A clean, friendly landing page for dog lovers.


Browse dogs by breed, suggest your own, and personalize your experience.

Features We Want to Build

  • Browse a curated list of dogs with images

  • Suggest your own dog (name + photo)

Who Is It For?

  • Dog lovers who want to explore breeds
  • People curious about what a breed looks like
  • Anyone who wants to share their dog
